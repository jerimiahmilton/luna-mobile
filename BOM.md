## üìù Notes

1. **Bearings:** 608 bearings are standard skateboard bearings - very common and cheap. Buy a pack of 10, they're useful for many projects.

2. **Motor:** The exact motor model (B-LA009) may have variations. Key specs are:
   - Voltage: 3V (can run 1.5-6V)
   - Speed: ~150 RPM at 3V
   - Worm gear output (self-locking)
   - 4mm D-shaft output

3. **Filament:** PLA is fine for indoor use. PETG is more durable if you want extra strength.

4. **Power:** If using batteries, expect 20-40 hours of runtime depending on motor efficiency and battery quality.
